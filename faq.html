You have questions. We have answers. FAQ'S

What are types of recognition available?

There are several types of recognition available depending upon the type of donation given. These recognitions are mailed to the donor. Note that many countries may charge customs and VAT charges for the importation of plaques. These fees are the responsibility of the individual or club locally receiving the plaque.

May I charge my donation to my credit card?

Sure. We accept charitable donations charged to Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover credit cards. Donations can be made safely and instantly online. We do not accept credit card donations over the phone. If you need assistance making your donation online, please contact Donor Assistance.

Does the website submit the information in secure mode?

Yes. The 2020 Disaster relief website submits the information over Secure Socket Layer (SSL), provides a secure channel to exchange information in encrypted mode

How do I know about the status of my payment online?

After you make the payment at the netbanking interface of the concerned bank, the status of payment is shown at the following pages. In case the payment is successful, the same is displayed at status page as well as conveyed to the email address / mobile number given by you. In case you are still not able to know the status of your payment. You may click Transaction History and find out the status of your transaction.
